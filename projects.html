Science/Tech

Biotein

I co-founded Biotein in April of 2019. We are a startup focused on human aging.

Sub-projects:

  • A biological age test - We are working on measuring biological age with a protein-focused test. Different proteins change in concentration as humans age, and we want to find which proteins change most, and use that to give our customers a full picture of their health. We hope to release this soon (early 2023).
  • Reducing the impacts of brain aging - Biotein is currently designing research proposals with the goal of curing age-related neurodegeneration.

Alio Internship

I interned at Alio (formerly Graftworx), a biotechnology company focusing on improving dialysis treatment through continuous monitoring of patient vitals.

I programmed an algorithm to extract breathing rate from heart data, and created a patient portal.


Boyden Lab Internship

I am developing cost-effective machine learning tools to further understand aging-related disaease risks at the Boyden Lab at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology.

COVID-19 and Aging

I was the first author on a peer-reviewed and cited academic paper in the Aging journal on COVID-19 and its correlation with age (doi: 10.18632/aging.203442). My responsibilities included research, data analysis and writing, and I collaborated with the Gladyshev Lab at Harvard on this paper.
