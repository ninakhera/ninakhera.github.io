Biotein

I co-founded Biotein in April of 2019. We are a startup focused on human aging.

Sub-projects:

  • A biological age test - We are working on measuring biological age with a protein-focused test. Different proteins change in concentration as humans age, and we want to determine which ones change most, and use that information to give our customers a full picture of their health. We hope to release this soon (early 2023).
  • Reducing the impacts of brain aging - Biotein is currently designing research proposals with the ultimate goal of curing age-related neurodegeneration.
