Epibuddy

Hey! Epigenetic data is hard to analyze. I get it. I built this tool as a starting point to help you make sense of your data and perform some quick initial analyses.

Ensure the group variable is named 'group', and the cell type variable is named 'cell_type' and both are stored in the obs section.

Future goals:

Am hoping to expand this to incorporate other types of data, giving a more multiplexed set of recommendations
