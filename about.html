Who am I?

Hey!

Thank you for visiting my website.

My name is Nina and I am a teenager. The above question is one I ask myself every day, but I will provide a lens.

I am really passionate about improving human healthspan, which is the measure of how long we are healthy during our lifespan.

I realize that one of the biggest detriments to our healthspan is aging. So, I want to reduce its impacts, in particular age-related disease (dementias, Parkinson's, cardiovascular diseases, etc).

I am doing this through my work at Biotein, a startup I have been working on since April of 2019. If you want to learn more about my projects, you can find them here.
