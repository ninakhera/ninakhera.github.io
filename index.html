A bit about me

I am a teenage human longevity researcher who is interested in preventing age-related diseases, especially those related to brain aging. I have collaborated with the Gladyshev Lab and Boyden Lab on data analyses and machine learning-based projects. I have also worked with companies such as Lygenesis, Rubedo Life Sciences, and Alio on computation and website-development projects. I am an avid runner, poet, and violinist.
