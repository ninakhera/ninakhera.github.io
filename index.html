Hey! I’m Nina Khera, a 15-year-old longevity & genomics researcher.

I’m working on a ton of sick projects right now. My main focus is a trackable and low-cost biological age test (Biotein - bioteinresearch.ca).

I’ve worked with sick companies like Wealthsimple and Walmart, and have spoken at conferences throughout North America.

In the future, I aspire to be a human longevity researcher working with senescent cells and impactful diseases, such as Alzheimer's.

To stay up-to-date on what I’m doing, sign up for my monthly newsletter at https://ninakhera.substack.com/.
